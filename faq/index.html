Cookie Care
How should I store the cookies to keep them fresh?
Store your cookies in a dry place, away from direct sunlight, and in an airtight container.
Can I freeze the cookies?
Yes, freezing will not affect the taste or texture of the cookies.
How do I reheat or serve the cookies for the best taste?
For best results, reheat in an air fryer at 175°C for 5 minutes.
Can I customize cookie flavors or add toppings?
Our cookie flavors are fixed, and we do not offer customization. You can choose from the available options on our website.
